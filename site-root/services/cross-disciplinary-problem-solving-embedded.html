Embedded Systems Cross-Disciplinary Problem Solving

Bridging hardware, software, and business gaps in complex embedded product development

Description

Embedded system development frequently encounters integration challenges where hardware, software, and business requirements intersect. I specialize in diagnosing these cross-disciplinary issues, identifying root causes, and aligning team priorities to maintain project momentum. For example, when a medical device company experienced system failures due to mismatched hardware data output and software processing, I facilitated collaboration between hardware and software teams, analyzed the data flow, and implemented a solution for reliable, accurate data exchange.

Contact me to resolve your most critical embedded system integration challenges.
