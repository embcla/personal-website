About me

Welcome to my portfolio. I'm Claudio Carbone, a passionate and resourceful embedded engineer based in Italy. With 15+ years of experience in the industry, I specialize in custom solutions, blending business and technology, bringing solutions to market, addressing the needs of different stakeholders.

Understanding that a product is more than just an application or PCBA, I focus on a holistic approach to product development. From the initial concept to the final execution, I work closely with all stakeholders, understanding vision, needs, goals, constraints. Fostering collaboration and keeping processes in check, ensures that the end result reflects the vision, connects with customers, brings revenue, cuts costs.
